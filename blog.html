Improving Image Resolution

By Priyank Modi
|
Photoshop

This technique uses the concepts widely used in computer vision. It works only for bi-coloured images. The idea is to first reduce the sharpness of the image using the Guassian blur tool and then use curves to simply take the two colours to their respective ends so that they completely dominate, gicing a clear, better resolution image.

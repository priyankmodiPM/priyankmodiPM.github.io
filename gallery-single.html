The Black Road to Color

  • Date:May 5,2018
  • Category:Digital Art
  • Tags:doodle, digital, hand-drawn

The series 'The Black Road to Color' consists of an illustration where the main subject is kept B&W while everything else is filled with, mostly bright colours, but I'm working on the next one in the series based on a dark theme. The inspiration behind it was from a fair i went to and couldn't stop noticing how we forget about the miles of road in between and just look forward to the colorful fair. There's much more to the actual meaning but there's not enoug space :P
